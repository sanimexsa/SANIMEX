# theme-factory

Toolkit for styling artifacts with thematic consistency across colors and typography.

## Pre-set Themes (Inspiration)
1. **Ocean Depths** - Professional maritime.
2. **Modern Minimalist** - Grayscale contemporary.
3. **Tech Innovation** - Bold modern tech.
4. **Midnight Galaxy** - Cosmic deep tones.

## Custom Theme Generation
- Always choose colors and fonts that represent the identity of the project.
- Ensure proper contrast and accessibility.
- Maintain theme identity across all screens and components.
